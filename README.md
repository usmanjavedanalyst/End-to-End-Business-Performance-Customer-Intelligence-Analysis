#  **End-to-End Business Performance & Customer Intelligence Analysis**

---

## **Project Overview**

This project demonstrates a **full-cycle business analytics workflow**, simulating the role of a **Junior Data Analyst** in a real organization.

The objective was to transform raw business data into **actionable insights** by analyzing sales performance, profitability trends, customer behavior, and regional performance — and to present these insights in a clear, **management-ready format**.

---

## **Business Objectives**

- Identify why **profit is declining despite sales growth**
- Determine **high-value customers** and retention opportunities
- Evaluate **product-level performance and profitability**
- Analyze **regional and seasonal trends**
- Provide **data-driven recommendations** to improve business outcomes

---

## **Tools & Technologies**

- **Excel** — Data validation, KPIs, pivot tables, summary analysis  
- **SQL (SQLite)** — Structured business queries and performance analysis  
- **Python (Jupyter Notebook)** — Data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), and visualization  
- **Power BI** — Interactive dashboards and DAX measures  
- **Canva** — Executive presentation and video walkthrough  

---

## **Project Workflow**

1. **Dataset understanding** and business assumptions  
2. **Data cleaning and validation** (Excel & Python)  
3. **SQL-based business analysis**  
4. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** using Python  
5. **Excel KPI reporting**  
6. **Power BI dashboard development**  
7. **Business insights and strategic recommendations**  
8. **Final executive presentation**

---

## **Key Deliverables**

- Cleaned and validated dataset  
- SQL analysis scripts and SQLite database  
- Python data cleaning & EDA notebook  
- Excel KPI dashboard  
- Power BI interactive dashboards  
- Business insights & recommendations document  
- **32-slide executive presentation (PDF)**  
- **Video presentation walkthrough**

---

## **Key Insights **

- Sales growth was largely driven by **high discounting**, negatively impacting profit margins  
- A **small percentage of customers** contributed a majority of total profit  
- Several products consistently **underperformed and generated losses**  
- Regional performance varied significantly across markets  
- Clear **seasonality patterns** influenced revenue and profitability
